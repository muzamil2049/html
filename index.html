Training

The first part of your day starts with an introduction to GoSkydive and what to expect from your tandem skydive. You will then receive specialist training in a simulated training environment from an experienced tandem master. GoSkydive is the only skydiving centre in the world to offer this level of training. Safety is at the core of the GoSkydive brand and this development takes the level of training over and above that of any other tandem skydiving dropzone in the world.

About

Our philosophy is customer first with everything we do, we want your skydive experience to be the best it can be, as our founder Gordon say’s “Because it’s more than just a jump”. From the moment you arrive to the moment you leave, everything we do at our drop zones is based around putting you at ease, and focusing on getting the most enjoyment from the experience that you have signed up for. We understand for some people a tandem skydive can seem a little overwhelming at first but trust us, over the years we have been operating and the thousands of customers we have jumped, we’ve yet to have one who didn’t love the thrill of and sense of accomplishment that skydiving affords you, you never know you might just get the bug!
